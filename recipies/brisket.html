Brisket Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Nothing pleases a crowd more than expertly cooked brisket.

With this recipie, ad the right amount of patience, anyone can cook a brisket like a true Texan pitmaster.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole brisket
  • charcoal, wood, or pellet smoker
  • BBQ seasoning of your choice
  • Coarse salt, pepper, and garlic
  • butcher paper
  • drink cooler

Instructions

  1. Trim the fat from the brisket if not already done.
  2. Season liberally with your seasoning (you can never add enough).
  3. Season further with a top coat of salt,pepper, and garlic.
  4. Let rest for 1-2 hours at room temperature.
  5. Set smoker to 200 degrees F and place brisket fat side down.
  6. Let the smoker work until brisket is at 160-170 degrees internal.
  7. Wrap in butcher paper and raise grill temperature to 225 degrees.
  8. Let it cook till 203 degrees internal temperature.
  9. Remove from smoker and place in cooler for 2-4 hours rest.
  10. Slice and serve.
